Dr. Jamal Nazrul Islam

The man who thought differently

Here's a time line of Dr. Jamal Nazrul Islam's life:

  • 1939 - Born in Jhenidah, Bangladesh
  • 1967-1971 - Attend the University of Cambridge. And, received a degree on Astronomy
  • 2003 - He was awarded Ekushey Padak by the Government of Bangladesh.
  • 2006 -He was made Professor Emeritus at the University of Chittagong
  • 2013 - dies at the age of 74.
